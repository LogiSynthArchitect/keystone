How it works

  • Closed: A compact field with underline border + icon + "Select service type" placeholder — matches the Customer step input style.
  • Tap: Opens a bottom sheet drawer listing all service types with icons + checkmark on selected.
  • Selected: Drawer closes. Field shows the chosen name as a gold chip with ✕ to clear.
  • Re-open: Tap the field again — drawer opens to the same list, current selection highlighted.
