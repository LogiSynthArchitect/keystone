How should the "drawer" work?

I see two ways to make Expenses, Parts Used, and other sections open like drawers

Option A — Collapsible inline panels

Tap the section header → it expands/collapses inline on the page. Like an Accordion.

▼ HARDWARE ITEMS 2 items • GHS 340.00
▶ EXPENSES 1 item • GHS 25.00
▶ PARTS USED 3 items

Shows a summary count when collapsed. Tap to expand and see full forms.

Option B — Tap opens a modal bottom sheet (like current inventory picker)

Tap "EXPENSES" → a drawer slides up from the bottom with the form. Like the inventory picker.

EXPENSES
Transport GHS 25.00
Troski fare to site

Each section is a separate bottom sheet. Cleaner, but requires more taps.
